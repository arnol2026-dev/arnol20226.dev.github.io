# 🧠 Critical Thinking and Media Literacy

In today's digital world, information is everywhere. People use social media, websites, and news platforms to stay informed. However, not all information is true or reliable.

Critical thinking is the ability to analyze information carefully before accepting it as true. It helps us make better decisions and avoid misinformation.

Media literacy is also very important. It means understanding how media works and how information is created and shared. This allows us to identify fake news and unreliable sources.

For example, before believing a news article, we should ask:
- Is the source trustworthy?
- Is the information supported by evidence?
- Is it biased or objective?

In my opinion, critical thinking and media literacy are essential skills in modern society. They help people become more informed, responsible, and independent.
